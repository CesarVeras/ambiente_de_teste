LPDS

The Digital Signal Processing Research Laboratory (LPDS) is a research lab in the Department of Electrical Engineering, Technology Center, at Federal University of Santa Catarina. Established in 1998, our laboratory conducts research in digital signal processing with emphasis on adaptive and statistical signal processing, image processing and data compression. The LPDS is highly involved in research and teaching activities at graduate and undergraduate levels. Members of the laboratory include faculty, graduate students and undergraduate students. The lab activities are supported with funds from industry and funding agencies (Capes, CNPq, FINEP, Fapesc, UFSC).

Mission

To conduct teaching, scientific research and development activities in digital signal processing to address the Brazilian society needs and demands, and to educate independent researchers with strong theoretical knowledge that can be used to solve a wide range of engineering problems.
